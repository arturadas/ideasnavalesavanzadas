About Us

Ideas

Unlike other companies, Ideas Navales Avanzadas (INA) has grown from the idea of a new way of looking at business and engineering based on the value of human contribution. Our work has been applauded by all our customers because of the extensive experience and skill of our team. We are ready to work from our customer's facility if deemed appropriate for better job performance and an improved product.

Navales

INA offers much more than staff, it provides extensive experience in all fields of the shipbuilding industry with the ability to generate alternative ideas and solutions for a wide variety of problems. The experience of having worked in many different environments, both in shipyards and design offices, enables us to analyse problems from different angles and to achieve the best and most cost effective solution.

Avanzadas

Development Engineering, Basic Engineering, Production Engineering, Inspection and Work Production Procedures... Our Engineers are not only able to develop the engineering for the final product manufacturing processes; they can also provide support as consultants during the production process, final inspection and compliance with current regulations.
